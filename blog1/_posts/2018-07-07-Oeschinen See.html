Oeschinen See Hiking

It has been said that Oeschinen See is the most beautiful mountain lake. It is a diomand in the middle of snow mountains. I went alone this hiking on a beautiful cloudy Saturady morning. I arrived at Kandersteg first, amazingly found that there is a traditional equestrian festival, which takes place on the second weekend of July every year there. It is competitive showjumping in a unique setting with horse riders.


After the festival, I walked around 10 mins and arrived the place where the cable car for Oeschinen See. It took 15 CHF for the cable car, even though I have a GA similar day ticket. So I decided to hike up from Kandersteg. It is truely amazingly worth it (See photos below). It only took around 1 hour to arrive at Oeschinen See, which is truly a diomand. You can go boating (15CHF for half an hour), swiming (a little bit cold), fishing (36 CHF for half a day), BBQ etc. there. But the most amazing for me is hiking there. Start from Oeschinen See, you can go to the alpine hiking rout, with amazing atomasphere of fantastic mountains.
