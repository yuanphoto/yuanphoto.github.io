what is interaction?

Interaction in statistics means the effect come from the targets only in this special moments. Once there are any elements changed, interaction will change. In figure, you will see two lines interact together instead of parallel, like "X". So we use "InteraXion" as our brand. InteraXion photography aims to recorod the interaction moments, which shows the emotions, stories at that time in that location. This is unique. Either the interactions of lovers, families, participants, or the interactions in nature, from plants and insects, can show you the diverse and interesting stories in the world.
